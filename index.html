About PESA

PESA (Planet Earth’s Student Army) is a Colorado public benefit corporation created to provide global collaboration tools and infrastructure to students, instructors, climate experts, multinational corporations, governments, nonprofits, and climate change volunteers of any age at no cost. Launching in the U.S. and Vietnam on Monday, January 23, 2023, PESA’s app (on Apple’s App Store as “PESA.Earth”) will be available in all 196 countries. Learning from organizations like the Peace Corp, Red Cross, Salvation Army, and the Duke of Edinburgh’s Award program, PESA combines successful aspects of older organizations with significant advances in technology allowing volunteers to create, collaborate, share knowledge, and implement climate solutions with others across the globe. To prepare for our 1/23/2023 launch, PESA is currently working with climate scientists across the globe to help prioritize where, when and which solutions should be implemented by businesses, governments, students (middle school, high school, and university) and other organizations.

Why PESA

PESA’s mission is to provide clean air, clean water, and a healthier planet to future generations. To fulfill our mission, governments, national/multi-national corporations, NGOs and universities across our planet must work together to create effective solutions to reverse global warming. PESA members will organize and provide tools to local communities in over 190 countries who will execute projects needed to meet 2030 and 2050 goals. PESA provides video discussion forums, solution/project plans for teams and solo projects, curated climate content, project management tools, “Green Habits”, and tracking of individuals, businesses, NPOs, NGOs and countries’ Climate Impact Score.
